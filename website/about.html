Medicine Hat Alberta
By Sierra Hill

Sierra Hill had always hated racist Medicine Hat Alberta with its modern,many mountains. It was a place where she felt sad. She was a stinky, smelly, soda drinker with pretty hands and small ears. Her friends saw her as a disturbed, dizzy devil. Once, she had even revived a dying duck. That's the sort of girl she was. Sierra walked over to the window and reflected on her stinky surroundings. The smelly teased like crawling cats. Then she saw something in the distance, or rather someone. It was the figure of Lauren Hill. Lauren was a dumb brute with stinky hands and smelly ears. Sierra gulped. She was not prepared for Lauren. As Sierra stepped outside and Lauren came closer, she could see the lonely glint in her eye. Lauren glared with all the wrath of 3480 stupid curious cows. She said, in hushed tones, "I hate you and I want a cookie." Sierra looked back, even more giggly and still fingering the sexy apple. "Lauren, you smell," she replied. They looked at each other with angry feelings, like two breezy, broad birds screaming at a very idiotic Thomas's funeral, which had punk rock music playing in the background and two dumbass uncles dying to the beat. Suddenly, Lauren lunged forward and tried to punch Sierra in the face. Quickly, Sierra grabbed the sexy apple and brought it down on Lauren's skull. Lauren's stinky hands trembled and her smelly ears wobbled. She looked furious, her emotions raw like a chilly, cuddly cookie. Then she let out an agonising groan and collapsed onto the ground. Moments later Lauren Hill was dead. Sierra Hill went back inside and made herself a nice drink of soda.

THE END
